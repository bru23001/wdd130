Overview

Purpose

To showcase the rafting services, prices, locations, and testimonials from previous customers. To attract new customers by providing information about the benefits of rafting, the safety measures we take, and the fun and adventure they can expect. To increase our bookings and revenue by allowing customers to make online reservations, payments, and cancellations. To build a loyal customer base by offering discounts, coupons, loyalty programs, newsletters, and social media integration. To enhance your brand image and reputation by sharing your story, mission, values, and achievements.

Audience

-Who are the target customer? Our target audience is people who are looking for an exciting, adventurous, and memorable outdoor activity. They are typically between 18 and 45 years old, have a moderate to high income level, and enjoy nature, sports, and challenges. They are also environmentally conscious and respectful of the natural resources and wildlife they encounter. -What do they want? They value fun, thrill, and camaraderie over comfort and convenience.They are not afraid of getting wet, dirty, or bruised. -How will my audience usually access my site? They usually use desktop and mobile device.
